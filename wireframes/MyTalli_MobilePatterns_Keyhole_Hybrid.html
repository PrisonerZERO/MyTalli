The Formula

  • Summary cards with mini charts (the "how am I doing?" answer)
  • Platform-specific stats on spokes (Avg. Sale, fee rate, payout status)
  • 5 most recent records as phone-native cards (the "what happened?" answer)
  • Clear desktop CTA at the bottom (the honest boundary)

Hub vs Spoke Differences

  • Hub shows platform breakdown bars + platform nav cards + cross-platform recent activity (with platform dots)
  • Spoke shows platform-specific stats (avg. sale, fee rate, payout info) + filtered recent activity (no platform dots needed — you already know where you are)
  • Both have the same structure: graphs → 5 cards → desktop CTA

Why This Works

  • The 5-card cap sets a clear expectation — this isn't a paginated list
  • Each spoke feels distinct (Etsy orange charts, Stripe purple charts, platform-specific stat labels)
  • Scrolls vertically only — no horizontal surprises
  • Desktop CTA is helpful, not blocking — you've already seen useful data above it
